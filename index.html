About me

Hey! I’m Priyadharshini. I work as a Frontend Developer and UI/UX Designer, and I honestly love what I do. I spend most of my time designing clean interfaces, building websites and figuring out how to make things feel simple and smooth. I’m that person who gets excited over good color palettes, neat layouts and a strong cup of coffee.

I like taking ideas and turning them into something people actually enjoy using. I sketch, design, prototype and then build the whole thing with a lot of patience and a little chaos. My style is pretty minimal and clean, and I always try to make my work feel easy, friendly and comfortable to use. If something I create makes someone smile or feel less confused, that’s a win for me.
